What it tracks

Four columns of a link program, finally in one place.

Each one usually lives in its own spreadsheet or someone's head. LinkLedger keeps them reconciled.

Exchanges

Log every link swap with owner, anchor, target URL, and DR. See live, pending, and lost at a glance — and skip the reciprocal deals search engines discount.

Outreach pipeline

Move prospects from pitch to placement without losing the thread. Email drafts, follow-up reminders, and who-said-what, attached to the record instead of buried in an inbox.

Link-health checks

Know the moment a hard-won link goes 404, nofollow, or quietly disappears — so you can recover it before a rankings dip tells you first.

AI-search visibility

Track whether AI answers and overviews are citing your pages, not just where you rank in blue links. Watch the GEO side of search alongside the classic one.

Shared by owner

Every row has an owner. See who's carrying the program, balance the load, and never lose a relationship when someone's out or moves on.

One source of truth

Search Console, Ahrefs, and your own data reconciled into a single dashboard — so the number you report is the number that's real.

How it works

From scattered sheets to a reconciled ledger.

  1. 01

    Bring your links in

    Import exchanges from a sheet, pull competitor gaps from an Ahrefs export, and connect Search Console. The ledger fills itself in.

  2. 02

    Work the pipeline

    Assign owners, draft outreach, set follow-up reminders, and move prospects toward live placements — every step on the record.

  3. 03

    Watch the balance

    Health checks flag links that break, and the dashboard shows live-versus-total at all times. Report numbers you can stand behind.
